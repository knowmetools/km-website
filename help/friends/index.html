Friends App FAQ

Watching Videos

How do I watch a video?

Simply tap on the Friend’s face and the video will fill the screen and play.

My videos are playing without sound!

Make sure your iPad is not muted. If it has a mute switch on the side, make sure the switch isn’t set to orange. If it doesn’t have a mute switch, swipe up from the bottom of the screen to show the Control Center, and make sure the mute icon (a bell) is not selected.

How do I switch between groups of friends?

You can either swipe the pages of friends left and right, or tap the name of the group at the top.

Adding Videos

How do I record a new Friend video?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Add Friend button at the left. Tap the big camera icon and choose “Record Video” to open the video camera. You can tap the white camera icon at the upper right to toggle between the selfie camera and the outward-facing camera. Tap the red circle to start and stop recording. After you stop recording, you can review the clip and either save it or re-record. Next, type the friend’s name in the Name field, an optional role in the Role field, and choose the appropriate group from the Group menu. Finally, tap Save & Add at the upper right.

What are some tips for recording a good video?

  • Begin by introducing yourself: say hello and your name.

  • Talk about a special activity or memory you share with your friend.

  • Keep it short, but not too short: 10-20 seconds is best.

  • If you can, ask someone to film you while holding the iPad about an arm’s distance away from your face.

  • Rehearsing will help you sound more natural and conversational. You can always re-record if you don’t like the first take.

How do I add a video that someone sent to me?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Add Friend button at the left. Tap the big camera icon and choose “Video Library”. Scroll through the list of videos saved to your iPad’s camera and choose the one you want. Next, type the friend’s name in the Name field, an optional role in the Role field, and choose the appropriate group from the Group menu. Finally, tap Save & Add at the upper right.

Editing Friends Videos

How do I change a friend's name?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode. Go to the group where your friend’s video is and tap on the Friend’s name. Type the new name into the Name field and tap Save at the upper right.

How do I change a friend's role?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode. Go to the group where your friend’s video is and tap on the Friend’s name. Type the new role into the Role field and tap Save at the upper right.

How do I change which group a friend is in?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode. Go to the group where your friend’s video is and tap on the Friend’s name. Choose a new group from the Group menu and tap Save at the upper right.

How do I delete a video?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode. Go to the group where your friend’s video is and tap on the red X at the upper right of the Friends video. Confirm that you want to delete the video and it will be removed.

Can I change the video used for one of my friends?

No. You will need to delete the video and record a new one. Sorry!

Managing Groups

How do I add a new group?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Edit Groups button at the left. Tap New Group, type the group’s name into the Group Name field, choose a color from the color menu, and tap Create at the upper right.

How do I change a group's name?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Edit Groups button at the left. Then tap on the group’s name, type the new name into the Group Name field, and tap Done at the upper right.

How do I change a group's color?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Edit Groups button at the left. Then tap on the group’s name, choose a new color from the color menu, and tap Done at the upper right.

How do I change which Friend is featured in a group's button?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap Manage Groups at the upper left. Then tap on the group’s name, tap the Friend you want from the Key Photo list, and tap Done at the upper right.

How do I change the order of groups?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Edit Groups button at the left. Then tap Edit at the upper left, and drag the three lines to the right of a group’s color up and down to change its order. Once you are satisfied, tap Done at the upper left.

How do I delete a group?

Tap the gear icon to enter Editing Mode, then tap the Edit Groups button at the left. Then tap Edit at the upper left, tap the red circle next to the group’s name, and tap delete. Once you are finished, tap Done at the upper left.

Troubleshooting

I can’t watch videos! When I tap one, it takes me to an edit screen.

You’re likely in Editing Mode, signified by a white bar across the top of the screen. Tap “Done” at the right end of the white bar to leave Editing Mode and you can watch videos again.

It won't let me record a video!

You may have denied the app permission to use your iPad’s cameras. Go to the Settings app on your iPad, choose Privacy in the left column, and tap Camera. Make sure the switch next to Friends is enabled (showing green).

My videos record without sound!

You may have denied the app permission to use your iPad’s microphones. Go to the Settings app on your iPad, choose Privacy in the left column, and tap Microphone. Make sure the switch next to Friends is enabled (showing green).
