Creating tools that people with communication difficulties can use to connect with others and make their wishes known.

Current

Know Me Tools syncs your most up-to-date content to the cloud, creating one "true version" accessible to all who need it.

Collaborative

Assign responsibility for updating content to the people with the best information, in real time, from their own devices.

Compatible

Our current version is only for iPads, but iPhone and Android releases are coming soon.

Customizable

Create content tailored to your needs, wants, favorite activities, daily routines, and more.

Offline

Changes made while offline will automatically sync the next time you connect.

Support

Our tutorials and Q&A will get you oriented, but when extra help is needed our team will be there to promptly answer questions. We learn something important every time we talk to a client and will do our best to give you efficient, caring service.
