Creating tools that people with communication difficulties can use to connect with others and make their wishes known.

Current

Know Me tools sync your most up-to-date content to the cloud, creating one "true version" accessible to all who need it.

Collaborative

Assign responsibility for updating content to the people with the best information, in real time, from their own devices.

Compatible

Our current version is only for iPads, but iPhone and Android releases are coming soon.

Customizable

Create content tailored to your needs, wants, favorite activities, daily routines, and more.

Offline

Make changes even while your device is offline and they will automatically sync the next time you connect.

Support

Tutorials and Q&A are available. When extra help is needed we will be there to assist. We learn something important every time we talk to a client.

Contact Us

If someone you care about might benefit from Know Me, tell us your story.
