What We Do

Know Me creates tools that help people with communication disabilities connect with others and be more clearly heard. Our larger goal is to provide both them and their teams with a system of tools and support that will increase independence, improve health, and build closer relationships – and ultimately a higher quality of life for both them and their teams.

Susan Hollobaugh

Enterprise Development

Susan’s roots are in Detroit. She was a teacher, an HR manager, and consultant before founding Know Me, inspired by her son Alex and others who have trouble communicating their full personhood. She says, “At some point I realized that the most important thing I could do was learn how to know Alex better and help others know him when I’m not around. I knew any solutions had to be fairly easy to implement and sustainable.” Susan loves a good puzzle and says this has been the most intriguing and challenging one. She looks forward to collaborating with others who are working on its solutions.

Michelle Neumann

Organizational Development and Client Growth

Michelle made her way to North Carolina by way of North Dakota, Illinois, and Colorado. After finishing her Master in Social Work (MSW) with a focus on organizational leadership and policy practice, she excitedly joined the Know Me team in 2016. Her person-centered training, along with her passion for creating widespread systemic change, has provided her the skills necessary to manage organizational operations, development, client growth, and other diverse jobs within the company. “Every person deserves to have their stories known,” she says. “Know Me is working to be a piece of important systemic change by providing tools for individuals who have a difficult time with being known... This is such a perfect way to change the world!”

Along with creating change for oppressed communities, Michelle is passionate about living mindfully through yoga, meditation, reading, and finding ways to enjoy life every day.
