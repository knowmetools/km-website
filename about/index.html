What We Do

Know Me is dedicated to helping people with communication disabilities connect with others and make their voices heard. Our larger goal is to help clients gain independence and better health, make their own choices and build closer relationships to sustain a high quality of life.

Our initial offerings are easy-to-use apps for people with communication disabilities and their support teams. These tools are designed to help people with communication disabilities express their unique personalities and convey their wishes and needs. They also facilitate team collaboration.
