What We Do

Know Me creates tools that help people with communication disabilities connect with others and be more clearly heard. Our larger goal is to provide both them and their teams with a system of tools and support that will increase independence, improve health, and build closer relationships – and ultimately a higher quality of life for both them and their teams.

Susan Hollobaugh

CEO

Susan’s roots are in Detroit. She was a teacher, an HR manager and consultant before founding Know Me, inspired by her son Alex and others who have trouble communicating their full personhood. “At some point I realized that I needed to know him better and to help others know him when I’m not around...and that any solutions had to be fairly easy and sustainable.” Her journey taught her the many reasons people can fail to be known; but also that there are a lot of good and wise people out there creating. Finding solutions. “This is about giving people the tools they need to understand those who aren’t easily understood,” she says. “Alex has a dimension that is hard to put into words it’s so extraordinary. People that get close to him tend to treasure the relationship. This population can give us so much, if we just know how receive it.” Susan is happy to be part of the team working on solutions for this puzzle.

Business Manager

Lauren Faulkenberry

Writer

Lauren is originally from Kershaw, South Carolina. Her passion for writing and storytelling has taken her all over the southeast, but she currently lives in Whittier, North Carolina. She has worked as an archaeologist, a teacher, and a park ranger, and has spent her life collecting and crafting stories. She joined the Know Me team in the autumn of 2015. “The people at Know Me are doing truly inspiring work,” she says. “It’s wonderful to see people so passionate about something that can make such a huge difference in people’s lives. I look forward to sharing their story with the world.”
