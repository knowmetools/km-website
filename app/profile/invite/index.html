You Have Been Invited to a Profile

Now available on iPad, iPhone, and iPod Touch from the App Store!

If a Browser other than Safari Opened

Please open the invitation in Safari. From your email, simply copy the invitation link (long press, then click "Copy") and then paste it into the Safari address bar (long press, then click "Paste").

From the Safari Web Browser

With your mobile device held vertically, pull down the webpage to reveal a banner at the top. Click the link in the banner. If your invitation doesn't show up within your Know Me app, click on the link in your email again.

If You Don't Have the Know Me App

If you don't have the app yet, just click the link below and you'll be taken to its page on the App Store.
