Accepting the Invite

To accept this invite, open the link you received in Safari on your iOS device.

Don't Have the App?

If you don't have the app yet, just click the link below and you'll be taken to its page in the App Store.

Once you've downloaded the app and created your account, click on the invitation link in the email you received and you'll be taken to the app.
