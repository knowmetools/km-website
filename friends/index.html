Friends

A Know Me App

The Friends app visually organizes the people in your life by group: family, friends, colleagues, teammates, classmates, and so on. You determine the categories. In early testing we found many unexpected uses for this seemingly simple app and expect to discover more. These are just a few uses:

  • Hear a personal message from someone in your life whenever you want, even when they are far away or busy.

  • Introduce your friends from one group with those from another.

  • Share information regarding how you relate to a variety of people.

  • Use as a name and face memorization tool for yourself and your team.

To use Friends, simply record video messages from your favorite people with your ipad or have them send you a recorded message. Arrange your recordings into groups you create.
