Friends

The Friends app was initially designed so people with limited communication ability could hear the voices of people they care about any time. It is also a great tool for memorizing names and faces.

{% include app-store-badge.html url="https://itunes.apple.com/us/app/friends-video-messages-from/id1101422660?ls=1&mt=8" %}

Organized

Organize and watch video clips from the people in your life by group: family, friends, colleagues, teammates, classmates, etc.

Personal

Hear a personal message from someone in your life whenever you want, even when they are far away or busy

Social Tool

Introduce your friends from one group to those from another and share information regarding how you relate to a variety of people

Learning Resource

Use as a name and face memorization tool for yourself and your team
